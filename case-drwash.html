The Challenge

One Generic Page For 11 Different Services

Dr. Wash & Clean is a commercial cleaning company operating in Cleveland, Ohio. They offered a wide range of cleaning services — from office cleaning to post-construction cleanup — but had no dedicated landing pages per service category. All services were crammed into a single generic page with no targeted copy or CTA strategy.

For a local service business relying on Google and paid traffic, this was a major conversion killer. A visitor searching "post-construction cleaning Cleveland" was landing on the same generic page as someone looking for office cleaning — with no relevance, no specific trust signals, and no tailored call to action.

My Role

GHL Page Builder + Conversion Copywriter

I was responsible for building all 11 service pages inside GoHighLevel — including layout design, section structure, conversion-focused copy, CTA placement, and mobile optimization. Each page was built to target a specific search intent and convert local commercial clients.

The Process

How Each Page Was Built

Step 01
Service Audit & Mapping
Audited all 11 service categories and mapped each to specific customer pain points, search intent, and decision triggers relevant to commercial clients in Cleveland.
Step 02
Page Structure Design
Designed a consistent but customizable page structure inside GHL — hero with service-specific headline, benefits, social proof section, FAQ, and a clear lead capture CTA.
Step 03
Conversion Copy Per Page
Wrote unique conversion copy for each of the 11 pages — tailored headlines, benefit-driven body copy, and CTAs aligned with the urgency level of each service type.
Step 04
GHL Build & Mobile Optimization
Built all pages in GoHighLevel page builder — optimized for mobile load speed, CTA button placement, and lead form integration. Used lazy loading and image compression best practices.
