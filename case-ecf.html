The Challenge

Meetings Were Falling Through the Cracks

ECF was losing potential clients at two key points in their pipeline: before meetings (no-shows due to lack of reminders) and after no-shows (no recovery follow-up in place). Staff were manually sending reminders when they remembered — which wasn't consistent — and when someone didn't show up, there was no system to re-engage them.

Additionally, no-show contacts were being manually assigned to staff, creating delays and gaps in follow-up. The entire post-booking experience was fragmented and dependent on human memory.

My Role

GHL Automation Architect

I designed and built two complete GoHighLevel automation workflows from scratch — mapping the trigger logic, delay sequences, email content, and staff assignment actions. Both workflows are now live and running on real contacts in the execution logs.

Workflow 1

ECF Meeting Booked + Reminder Series

This workflow triggers the moment a meeting is booked and sends a timed sequence of reminders to keep prospects engaged and reduce no-show rates.

Trigger
Meeting Booked
Workflow activates instantly when a contact books a meeting through the GHL calendar — no manual action required.
Day -3
Wait Until 3 Days Before
GHL waits until exactly 3 days before the scheduled meeting time, then sends the first confirmation & excitement email.
Day -1
24 Hour Reminder
A reminder email goes out 24 hours before the meeting — reinforcing the value of the call and what to expect.
-30 min
Final Reminder + Store Video
30 minutes before the meeting, a final reminder fires with a short store video to build excitement and reduce last-minute drop-offs. A 15-minute delay separates the video email from the reminder.

Workflow 2

ECF Staff Feedback + No-Show Recovery

This workflow handles contacts who didn't show up for their meeting — automatically assigning them to a staff member and sending a 3-email recovery sequence over 3 days.

Trigger
No-Show Detected
Workflow triggers when a contact misses their scheduled meeting — no manual tagging needed.
Step 1
Wait 30 mins → Assign Staff
A 30-minute wait fires, then the contact is automatically assigned to a staff member inside GHL CRM for human follow-up oversight.
Day 1
No-Show Email 1
First recovery email sent — empathetic tone, acknowledging they missed the call and offering to reschedule easily.
Day 2
No-Show Email 2
Second follow-up — adds value by sharing a resource or insight, keeping the conversation warm without being pushy.
Day 3
No-Show Email 3
Final recovery attempt — creates light urgency and provides one clear CTA to rebook. After this, the contact exits the workflow.
