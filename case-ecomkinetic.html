The Challenge

A US Wholesale Service With No Digital Acquisition System

Ecom Kinetic offers a done-for-you Amazon wholesale service to investors — a high-ticket offer requiring trust, credibility, and consistent lead flow. When they came to me, they had no functioning funnel, no landing page, and no automated lead nurturing in place. Leads were handled manually, follow-up was inconsistent, and there was no paid acquisition channel running.

The additional challenge: their target audience — potential Amazon investors — needed to be educated before converting. This wasn't an impulse purchase. The system had to warm leads, build trust, and qualify them before any sales call.

My Role

Full-Stack GHL Build + Meta Ads + AI Automation

I was brought in as the sole marketing system builder. My scope covered everything from landing page to ad creative to post-lead automation — a complete client acquisition pipeline architected and deployed inside GoHighLevel.

The Process

Step-by-Step System Build

Step 01
GHL Landing Page Build
Built a high-converting landing page at amz.ecomkinetic.com inside GoHighLevel — dark navy and gold brand aesthetic, mobile-optimized, with embedded GHL form (replacing slow calendar widget that was killing mobile conversions).
Step 02
Conversion Diagnosis
Diagnosed a zero-leads issue traced to slow mobile calendar load times. Implemented embedded GHL form solution — eliminating friction and restoring mobile lead capture completely.
Step 03
Meta Ads Campaign Build
Built and launched Facebook Messenger campaigns and lead gen funnels — ROI-driven creatives using market stats, case study angles, and FOMO-based copy. Generated 3+ qualified investor leads in the first campaign cycle.
Step 04
GHL Automation Workflows
Designed and deployed 3+ GHL automation workflows covering appointment booking, lead nurturing sequences, CST timezone calendar configuration, and automated follow-up — reducing manual work by an estimated 70%.
Step 05
AI Lead Finder Agent
Built an autonomous lead-finder agent using Make.com, SerpAPI, Groq AI, and Gmail — enabling one-prompt investor outreach automation. The agent researches, qualifies, and drafts personalized outreach without manual input.
Step 06
Campaign 2 & 3 Iterations
Analyzed campaign data across multiple cycles. Identified accidental edits that reset Facebook's learning phase and a rejected creative due to income claim violations. Carried winning creatives forward and added geo-targeting refinements in Campaign 3.
